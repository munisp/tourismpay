Offline NFC & CBDC Bridge

Payments where there's no internet. Settlements in central bank money.

Offline NFC Tap-to-Pay

Go service with Ed25519 cryptographic vouchers

Tourist pre-loads vouchers online
→
NFC tap at merchant (no internet)
→
Vouchers queue locally
→
Settle via TigerBeetle on reconnect
  • Ed25519 signed — cryptographic proof of payment
  • Double-spend protection via voucher serial numbers
  • Temporal workflows for reconciliation
  • Zero hardware cost — merchant's phone is the POS
